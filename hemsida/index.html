Instructions manual

Instructions on how to use your new Medkit device.

How to transfer the alarmtimes to your Medkit device:

  1. Attach the Medkit stationary device on your medicine container
  2. Download the application for the device and install it on your smartphone
  3. Put batteries in both Medkit devices.
  4. Open the installed application, and enter the time you wish for the Medkit to remind you.
  5. Enter the time of your choosing in the app, that you wish for the Medkit to set off the alarm
  6. The chose Transfer alarmtimes/Next
  7. When you see the NFC icon in the app, then you need to hold the smartphone agains the NFC marker on the device on your arm. Keep holding the smartphone as long as the LED bulb glows red.
  8. When the LED turns off, its safe to remove your smartphone from the Medkit device.
  9. Put on your Medkit on your arm
  10. Your done! The alarmtimes has been transfered and are stored in the device now!

How to stop the alarm when the Medkit is reminding you of your medicin:

  1. When the alarm is active on the device, walk over to your medicin container, where you have attached the Medkit stationary device to.
  2. Hold your Medkit device on your arm against the NFC marker on the Medkit stationary device.
  3. Keep holding the MedKit device on your arm against the Medkit stationary device as long as the LED buld is glowing red.
  4. When the LED bulb turns off, the alarm turns off and then your done!
